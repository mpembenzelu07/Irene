Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Pamoja Eats

How does multi-restaurant ordering work?

You can add dishes from different restaurants to a single cart. Our AI optimizes the delivery route and timing so all items arrive together or in the most efficient sequence. Each restaurant prepares their dishes independently, and our delivery network coordinates the final delivery to you.

How do I track orders from multiple restaurants?

You get real-time tracking for each dish in your order. The app shows you:
1. Preparation status at each restaurant
2. Pickup time by our delivery partner
3. Live location of the delivery person
4. Separate ETAs for items from different restaurants
You'll receive notifications at each stage of the process.

Can I schedule orders for later?

Yes! You can schedule orders for any future date and time. We also offer recurring orders - for example, you can set up lunch every weekday at 1 PM, or dinner every Friday at 7 PM. This is perfect for offices, busy professionals, or regular family meals.

How does the AI combo builder work?

Our AI analyzes your taste preferences, previous orders, and popular combinations to suggest perfect meal pairings from different restaurants. For example, it might suggest a main dish from one restaurant, sides from another, and drinks from a third - all based on complementary flavors and delivery optimization.

What cities do you operate in?

Currently we operate in Dar es Salaam, Arusha, and Mwanza. We're expanding to Dodoma and Zanzibar in the coming months, with plans to cover all major Tanzanian cities by the end of 2024.
