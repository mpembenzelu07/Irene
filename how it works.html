Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Pamoja Eats

How does multi-restaurant ordering work?

You can add dishes from different restaurants to a single cart. When you check out, we coordinate with all restaurants simultaneously. Our AI optimizes delivery routes so your food arrives as close together as possible. You pay once for everything.

Can I customize dishes from different restaurants?

Yes! Each dish can be customized individually - spice level, portion size, add-ons, special instructions. Each restaurant receives your customized order exactly as you specified.

How are delivery times calculated for multi-restaurant orders?

Our AI calculates the optimal delivery route considering preparation times at each restaurant, distance, and traffic. We show you an ETA for each dish and coordinate delivery to minimize wait times between different restaurant items.

What if one restaurant in my order is delayed?

You'll receive real-time notifications if any item is delayed. Our system can adjust delivery routing dynamically. In rare cases, we may deliver items separately with compensation for the inconvenience.

How do I split payments for group orders?

When you start a group order, you get a shareable link. Friends can join and add their items. At checkout, the bill splits automatically based on what each person ordered. Everyone pays their share directly through the app.
