How Pamoja Eats Works

Get your favorite meals in 4 simple steps

1

Sign Up & Set Preferences

Create your profile and tell us about your taste preferences, dietary restrictions, and favorite cuisines.

2

Browse & Build Your Cart

Select dishes from multiple restaurants, customize each item, and add them to your multi-restaurant cart.

3

Checkout & Pay

Pay via M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, Airtel Money, card, or wallet. Schedule delivery or set up recurring orders.

4

Track & Enjoy

Track each dish in real-time, receive live updates, and enjoy your meal from multiple restaurants!
