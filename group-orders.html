Group Orders Made Easy

Share orders with friends, family, or colleagues and split payments automatically. Perfect for offices, parties, and social gatherings.

How Group Orders Work

Create a group order in 4 simple steps and invite others to join.

1

Create Group

Start a group order and set preferences like delivery time, restaurant choices, and payment method.

2

Share Link

Share a unique link with friends via WhatsApp, email, or social media. No app download needed for guests.

3

Everyone Orders

Each person adds their items from multiple restaurants. See real-time updates of what others are ordering.

4

Auto-Split Checkout

When ready, checkout automatically splits the bill. Everyone pays their share directly.
