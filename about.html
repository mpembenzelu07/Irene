Our Mission & Vision

Empowering Local Communities

Founded in 2023, Pamoja Eats emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to create a sustainable, equitable food distribution network that benefits every stakeholder in Tanzania's food chain.

We believe in harnessing technology to solve real-world challenges while preserving the rich culinary heritage of Tanzania.

Our 2025 Vision

To become East Africa's leading food ecosystem platform, connecting 10,000+ local producers with 500,000+ consumers while reducing food waste by 40% and increasing farmer incomes by 60%.
