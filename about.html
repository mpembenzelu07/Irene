Our Story

Pamoja Eats was born from a simple idea: to revolutionize how Tanzania experiences food delivery by combining technology with authentic culinary traditions.

Our Mission & Vision

Driving innovation in Tanzania's food ecosystem

Our Mission

To create Tanzania's first AI-powered digital food ecosystem that connects multiple restaurants, empowers local businesses, and delivers unique, personalized dining experiences through innovative technology.

Our Vision

To become East Africa's leading food technology platform, transforming how people discover, order, and enjoy food while boosting restaurant revenues and creating thousands of opportunities across the supply chain.

Our Journey

From idea to Tanzania's leading food ecosystem

2022 Q3: Concept Born

Irene and George identified the gap in Tanzania's food delivery market. Initial concept of multi-restaurant ordering developed.

2022 Q4: I&G Technologies Onboard

Development team began building the AI-powered platform prototype. First restaurant partnerships secured.

2023 Q1: MVP Launch

Pilot launched in Dar es Salaam with 10 partner restaurants. Initial user testing and feedback collection.

2023 Q2: AI Features Added

Introduced Taste Adventure Game, AI Combo Builder, and multi-restaurant cart functionality.

2023 Q3: Expansion Phase

Expanded to Arusha and Mwanza. Reached 50+ restaurant partners and 10,000+ active users.

2023 Q4: Future Vision

Planning East Africa expansion. Developing B2B solutions and sustainable delivery initiatives.
