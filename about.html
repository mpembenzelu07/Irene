Our Story

Building Tanzania's first digital food ecosystem where technology meets tradition, and innovation serves community.

Our Mission & Vision

At Pamoja Eats, we believe food should bring people together. Our mission is to revolutionize how Tanzania experiences food delivery by creating connections between restaurants, customers, and communities.

We envision a future where technology enhances culinary experiences, where small restaurants can compete with large chains, and where customers can enjoy diverse cuisines from multiple kitchens in one seamless order.

"Your Taste, Your Way" isn't just our tagline - it's our promise to every customer and restaurant partner.

Our Core Belief

Food is more than sustenance - it's culture, connection, and community. We're building technology that celebrates Tanzania's rich culinary heritage while embracing the future.

Our Journey

From idea to Tanzania's fastest-growing food ecosystem

The Idea is Born

CEO Irene Mgimba and Director George William identified a gap in Tanzania's food delivery market. While living in Dar es Salaam, they realized no platform allowed ordering from multiple restaurants simultaneously. The concept for Pamoja Eats was born.

2021

I&G Technologies Founded

The technical foundation was established. A team of Tanzanian developers, designers, and food industry experts came together to build the first version of the Pamoja Eats platform, focusing on multi-restaurant cart technology.

2022

Pilot Launch in Dar es Salaam

We launched with 10 partner restaurants in Dar es Salaam. The response was overwhelming - customers loved the ability to mix dishes from different restaurants. Our AI taste matching feature quickly became a favorite.

2022

Expansion Across Tanzania

With successful proof of concept, we expanded to Mwanza, Arusha, and Zanzibar. Our restaurant partnerships grew to over 200 establishments across Tanzania's major cities.

2023

The Future - Beyond Borders

Today, we're working on expanding across East Africa while continuously innovating our platform with AI features, sustainability initiatives, and enhanced restaurant tools.

2024+
