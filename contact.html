Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I track my order from multiple restaurants?

You can track each item in your order separately in the app. Go to "My Orders" and select your current order. You'll see separate tracking for each restaurant item, with live updates on preparation status and delivery ETAs.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept MPesa, Tigo Pesa, Airtel Money, credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard), and our Pamoja Wallet. You can also split payments in group orders.

How do I become a restaurant partner?

Visit our For Restaurants page or fill out the restaurant partnership form above. Our partnership team will contact you within 24 hours to discuss onboarding and setup.

How does the loyalty program work?

You earn 1 point for every TSh 1,000 spent. Points can be redeemed for discounts, free items, or exclusive deals. You also unlock badges for achievements like frequent ordering or trying new restaurants.

What cities do you currently operate in?

Currently we operate in Dar es Salaam, Arusha, and Mwanza. We're expanding to Dodoma and Zanzibar in the coming months.
