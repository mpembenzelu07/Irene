Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I track my multi-restaurant order?

You can track each dish from different restaurants separately in the "My Orders" section of the app. We provide live updates for each item, including preparation status and delivery ETA. You'll also receive push notifications for important updates.

What if I need to cancel or modify my order?

You can modify or cancel your order within 5 minutes of placing it through the app. For modifications after this time, please contact our support team immediately at +255 789 123 456. Note that cancellation policies may vary by restaurant.

How do I report a problem with my delivery?

If there's an issue with your delivery, please contact our support team immediately. You can use the in-app chat, call +255 789 123 456, or email support@pamoja-eats.co.tz. Include your order number and details of the issue for faster resolution.

What are your customer support hours?

Our customer support team is available from 7:00 AM to 11:00 PM daily, including weekends and holidays. For urgent issues outside these hours, you can leave a message and we'll respond first thing in the morning.

How do I become a restaurant partner?

Visit our For Restaurants page to learn about partnership opportunities and fill out the application form. Our partnership team will contact you within 24 hours to discuss next steps.
